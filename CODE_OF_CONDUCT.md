# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a welcoming experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

**Positive behaviors include:**
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community

**Unacceptable behaviors include:**
- Trolling or derogatory comments
- Personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct, or ban contributors for inappropriate behavior.
